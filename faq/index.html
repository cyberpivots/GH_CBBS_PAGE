FAQ

Common questions

CBBS combines off-grid message records, field nodes, operator consoles, and local display surfaces.

What is CBBS?

CBBS is an off-grid bulletin board and field-operations mesh built around durable records, local access, LoRa-backed communication paths, operator review, and field-visible displays.

What do current, prototype, and roadmap labels mean?

CBBS is actively developing. Current system labels identify active surfaces, prototype labels identify display or interface previews, example scenario labels identify modeled workflows, and roadmap labels identify planned capability areas.

What is next for CBBS?

CBBS continues to mature across hub operations, room-node access, Windows workbench lanes, Nextion/P070 display surfaces, agriculture records, diagnostics, and field workflow examples.
