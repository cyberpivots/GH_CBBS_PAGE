# Bill Of Materials

Track non-printed parts needed for enclosure prototypes.

Candidate categories:
- Screws and nuts
- Heat-set inserts
- Standoffs
- Gaskets or foam seals
- Cable glands and strain relief parts
- Antenna pigtails, bulkhead connectors, and caps
- Display cover material
- Labels and QR/SSID plates
- Mounting hardware for wall, pole, rail, or desktop use

Do not mark any item as production-approved until it has fit, assembly, and sourcing notes.
